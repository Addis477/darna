The Darna Chicken Legacy

DARNA CHICKEN started with a simple belief: great food brings families together. Our secret spice blend has been passed down for generations, perfected in the heart of Addis Ababa at our 4 Kilo location.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily, using only premium ingredients and traditional cooking methods. From our signature shawarma to slow-roasted chicken, we pour love into every bite.

"Feel at home. Eat like a family." — that's not just a slogan, it's our promise to every customer who walks through our doors at 4 Kilo.
